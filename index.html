Hello Friends

My name is Jack, and this is my homepage. I'm a student, historian, musician, artist, and a couple more things that I'm probably not as committed to. This site is going to be for all my little interests and trivia, and I hope I can show someone something interesting :).

Check out the links to the left, where you can find most of my projects, and be sure to sign the guestbook! To the right you can find my beautiful potted plants, part of the webgarden project started by Miss Moss which you can read about here.

This site is free software! The license and source are available from the github repository, the link for which is to the left!

I make music and DJ as Gnomonia! Some of it is even ok! Feel free to ask for any of it! I'm always down to share.
